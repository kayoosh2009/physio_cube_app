Common questions about Physio Cube

We are the Physio Cube team, a new project dedicated to helping you get examined remotely by professional physiotherapists.
Our project works quite simply in a few steps:
  1. Get checked by your family doctor and receive approval for physiotherapy.
  2. Visit your gym (or any facility with our Cube).
  3. Complete the injury survey in the app.
  4. Take the test inside the Cube (strength checks, range of motion, etc.).
  5. Send the data to a physiotherapist and get a response (treatment plan or an in-person referral).
We make life easier. Instead of standing in long queues and paying high costs for minor injury consultations, we provide the same professional service, just faster and more convenient.
We officially work with physiotherapists from the Hapoel Be'er Sheva basketball team. Additionally, we study and utilize the most advanced data analysis techniques available.
Our physiotherapists are selected via a specialized algorithm. They are professionals who have their own clinics for patients, and our platform serves as a great way for them to earn extra income fully digitally.
